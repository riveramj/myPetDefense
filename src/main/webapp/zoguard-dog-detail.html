Product Label

ZoGuard Plus provides total control of both fleas and ticks. Classified as a "non-repellent," fleas and ticks cannot detect the presence of the active ingredients, ensuring a lethal dose is achieved.

    • Contains the same active ingredients used in Frontline Plus
    • Kills adult fleas, ticks, and chewing lice on contact
    • Protects against sarcoptic mange by controlling mites
    • Prevents flea re-infestation by killing flea eggs and larvae
    • Waterproof
